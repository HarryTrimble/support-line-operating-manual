Note taking

It is ok to make paper notes during a call. After every call the operator will need to record details on the caller and their support needs on an online form. Noting down key details during the conversation will help operators complete this as accurately as possible.

  • Check what notes to take
  • Write notes on paper
  • Record information from the call
  • Destroy your paper notes at the end of your shift
