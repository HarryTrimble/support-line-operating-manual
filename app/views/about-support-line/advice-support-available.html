Advice and support available through the support line

The support line is for people staying staying at home and finding it hard to access food or medication, or are feeling lonely or worried.

Advice and support includes:

  • a chat to one of our friendly Red Cross volunteers
  • emotional support
  • links to local organisations who may be able to help
  • information and practical advice.
  • Urgently needed food and medication
