Advice is needed

What you should do

  1. Call Safeguarding Assurance Team
  2. Explain your safeguarding concern to the team
  3. Action any advice given by the team. This may include referrals to social services, the police or GPs
  4. Get advised by the team if the incident needs reporting on Datix.
  5. Record information from the call
  6. Speak to your supervisor, if the call has affected you
! Warning Any allegation of abuse or harm against a British Red Cross representative must be reported to the Safeguarding Assurance Team promptly.
